About Us

GHPS DHUPADAKTE- BAIRAMPALLI is a Co-educational school located in the BRAHMAVARA block of UDUPI district in Karnataka. Established in 1994, it is managed by the Department of Education and serves students from class 1 to class 7. The school is located in a rural area. It does not have a pre-primary section. Kannada is the medium of instruction at GHPS DHUPADAKTE- BAIRAMPALLI. The school is accessible by all weather roads, and academic sessions begin in April.
